Department of Visual Media

Greetings from the Department of Visual Media!

We are the creative force behind the biggest events at BITS Pilani. Whether it's building sleek apps, designing stunning websites, producing high-octane promotional videos, or developing useful tools like the Timetable Generator, DVM's work is the gold standard.
With 8000+ users relying on our platforms and our unwavering commitment to excellence, DVM doesn't just meet expectations; we set new ones. Our work isn't just about tech—it's about creating experiences that people remember long after the fest is over.

To know more, visit our website at: https://bits-dvm.org/

Verticals

Frontend

We are the team behind DVM's incredible websites. From the official websites of Oasis and APOGEE to various useful interfaces such as the DVM Timetable Generator. We strive to make our websites flawless and make sure they captivate from the first click.

Tech Stack: NextJS, React, GSAP, ThreeJS, GLSL, SASS, TypeScript

Backend

The backend team manages servers, databases, integrating apps and websites with APIs for OASIS and APOGEE. We handle core logic for processes like registration and transactions during fests, and design the structure and algorithms behind projects like DVM Timetable generator.

Tech Stack: Python, Django, Postgres

App Development

We are the team behind the apps that make fest life easier—whether it's cashless payments, event schedules, or ordering food. We ensure that everything is just a tap away. When it comes to mobile experiences, we make sure they're unforgettable.

Tech Stack: Flutter

Design

Our design team is responsible for designing the user interfaces for apps and websites for fests and the graphics that go along with it. We also create posters and social media content, contributing to the overall visual communication and promotion of events.

Tech Stack: Figma, Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop

Video

We are the storytellers who set the tone for the fest, crafting everything from teasers to trailers. Our videos mesmerize the entire campus. Whether it's a theme reveal or a curtain raiser, we build anticipation like no other.
2D Team - We focus on producing high-quality 2D videos that effectively communicate ideas and tell stories through compelling visual content. Our team combines creativity with technical skills to create videos that stand apart.
3D Team - We specialize in bringing concepts to life through immersive 3D modeling, animation and visual effects. We are passionate about experimenting with a wide range of visual styles, pushing the boundaries of creativity to create unique and captivating visuals.

Tech Stack: Blender, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere Pro, Unreal Engine
