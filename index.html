Greetings from the Department of Visual Media!

We are the creative force behind the biggest events at BITS Pilani. Whether it's building sleek apps, designing stunning websites, producing high-octane promotional videos, or developing useful tools like the Timetable Generator, DVM's work is the gold standard.
